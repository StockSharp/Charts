How backfill works
  • The controller loads an initial page, then watches the visible range.
  • Scroll / drag left (or zoom out) toward the oldest loaded bar.
  • When the edge gets within the prefetch threshold, it asks the feed for the next older page and prepends it — no full reload.
  • The SMA overlay recomputes over the extended history as it arrives; in Footprint mode the same prepend feeds the order-flow bars.
  • Watch loaded climb from 300; more before flips to no at the start.
Feed

A fake in-memory datafeed of 2500 bars serves pages with a short latency so the loading… state is visible. Nothing hits the network.
