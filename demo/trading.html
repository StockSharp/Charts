‹ sschart Trading from the chart · M10 broker-agnostic layer
How it works
  • Drag any order line to a new price — an optimistic preview follows the cursor.
  • The chart emits a modify intent; the demo "broker" confirms or rejects after a moment.
  • On reject the line snaps back — the chart never mutates broker state itself.
  • Ctrl+click the chart to place a limit order (left=buy, right=sell).
  • The bracket (ENTRY / SL / TP) is one linked OCO group; the LONG position shows live P&L.
